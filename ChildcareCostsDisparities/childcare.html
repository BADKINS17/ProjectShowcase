Summary

Brief description: Analysis of childcare cost disparities across U.S. counties/states, leveraging cost‐of‐care data, socioeconomic indicators, and visualizations to highlight regional and demographic trends.

How to contribute

Contributions are welcome! Consider adding:
• Additional visualizations or dashboards (e.g., via Tableau)
• Updated data files (e.g., newer years of the National Database of Childcare Prices)
• Improved documentation in the README or supplemental write‐up of findings.
